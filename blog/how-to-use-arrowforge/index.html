How to Use ArrowForge — Full Walkthrough

▶️

Video coming soon

ArrowForge is a free, offline-first archery tool for iOS (and Android, coming mid-June 2026) that helps archers and bow techs build, tune, and analyze their setups with precision. This walkthrough covers everything you need to get started.

What's covered in this video

  • Setting up your bow profile — draw weight, draw length, and bow type
  • Configuring your arrow — shaft, point weight, nock, and fletching
  • Understanding FOC (Front of Center) and what the numbers mean
  • Spine recommendations and how to read them
  • Trajectory modeling — range, wind, and elevation
  • Pursuit effectiveness ratings for hunting scenarios
  • Using the AI features — autofill, recommender, and build score
  • Saving and managing multiple builds

ArrowForge is available free on the App Store. AI features and cloud backup are available with a Pro subscription.
